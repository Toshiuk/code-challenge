The Pilgrim riddle

  1. You already walked 2 blocks and your tax total now is 4.
  2. Your total increases by two for every block you walk east, and decreases by two for every block you walk west.
  3. Your total doubles for every block you walk south and is cut in half for every block you walk north.
  4. Your tax total during the journey may be either positive or negative, but must be zero when you reach temple.
  5. Once you have walked through a block you may not pass through it again in any direction.
  6. You may pass through the same intersection twice.
  7. You have no money and no way of earning it through any means outside of walking tax.
  8. Your tax due immediately upon arriving at the temple.
  • Use keyboard arrows to move.
  • Press "R" to restart.
